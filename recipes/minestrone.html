Minestrone

Description

Minestrone is a hearty Italian soup traditionally made with beans, pasta, and lots of vegetables. Enjoy the slow-cooked flavor in just about 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 3/4 cup diced carrot
  • 3/4 cup sliced celery
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 ounce) can cannelleni beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1/2 cup dry ditalini pasta
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh italian parsley
  • 1 tablespoon shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Saut√© onion, carrot, celery, garlic, and rosemary until vegetables begin to soften, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, beans, water, broth, zucchini, pasta, and pepper; bring to a simmer. Cook, uncovered and stirring occasionally, until pasta is tender, 10 minutes. Stir in parsley. Serve topped with Parmesan and additional parsley if desired.
